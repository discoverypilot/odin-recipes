Mac & Cheese

Description

Another great comfort food that you can cook in bulk for easy meals throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • 16oz pasta
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 8oz cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to box directions. Drain well & set aside.
  2. Use the same pot to melt the butter. Add flour and stir into a roux. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Slowly stir in milk to form a sauce base.
  4. Add cheese and stir until creamy. Then add pasta.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste. Might I also recommend a little bit of curry powder and some dried herbs for a little bit of flavor? Then serve.

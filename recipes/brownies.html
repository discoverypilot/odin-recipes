Description

A simple recipe for when you want something sweet and chocolatey :)

Ingredients

  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 8oz semi-sweet chocolate (chips or chopped)
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup walnuts, halved

Steps

  1. Toast the walnuts in a pan for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  2. Set the walnuts aside and cook butter over low/medium heat enough to brown the milk solids.
  3. Add the chocolate to the saucepan, melting and stirring until combined.
  4. preheat oven to 350 F and grease a 9" glass baking pan.
  5. whisk the sugars into the mixture and let the pan cool ~10min.
  6. Once it's cooled, add flour, cocoa powder, and salt, whisking until combined again. Then fold in walnuts.
  7. Pour the batter into the baking pan and bake for 30-35 min. Let cool in the pan and serve.
